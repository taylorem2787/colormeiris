colorme by iris

What color are you feeling at the moment? Discover on colorme, a photo browser whose color evolves with you!
As you browse photos, click on a thumbs up or thumbs down icon to rate a photo. colorme continually calibrates its color based on your color preference. Is colorme a mood ring app? Does it reflect your unconscious color attractions? Click on EXPLORE to find out!
